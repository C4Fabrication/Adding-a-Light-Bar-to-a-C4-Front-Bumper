Adding a Light Bar to a C4 Front Bumper

For the last twenty years, light bars have been a constant in the offroading world, and they’ve come a long way in that time. Integrated into most C4 bumpers is the provision to mount a single-row LED light bar snugly within the bumper itself.

  • Most bumpers are built to accommodate a 30” single-row light bar
  • The Baja Designs S8 is what C4 bracket designs are based off of, but others with similar dimensions can be made to fit. Testing has been done to confirm Rigid, Heretic and the Extreme X6S will fit in C4's new end tab mounting system.
  • Exceptions to the 30” standard are the LC250 Land Cruiser Lo-Pro (which fits a 20” single-row) and the 2022+ Tundra full-size bumpers (which fit a 40” single-row).
  • Bumpers not built to accommodate a light bar are the 2022+ Tundra Lo-Pro and the 2024+ Tacoma Lo-Pro, since their designs are incredibly sleek along with the Rock Runner series of minimalist winch bumpers.
Find Us Here!

The Baja Designs S8 light bars have become C4's standard regarding sizing and fitment and are available to purchase at the same time as a bumper. Also available are light bars from Heretic Studio which offer a slightly more accessible price while still being made in the USA using quality components, plus get an automatic 10% off when Heretic lights are packaged with a bumper!
